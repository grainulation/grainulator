How it works

Three steps. One compiled truth.

Every research sprint follows the same pipeline. Each stage strengthens the next.

1

Ask your question

Type anything. Grainulator extracts parameters, audience, and constraints — then searches for prior research automatically.

2

Research & challenge

Multiple angles explored in parallel. Every finding becomes a typed claim. Then /challenge stress-tests them adversarially.

3

Compile the brief

The compiler catches contradictions, flags weak evidence, and produces your decision document. Only what passes the gate ships.
