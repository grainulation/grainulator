How it works

Five stages. One truth.

Every research sprint flows through a battle-tested pipeline. Each stage strengthens the next.

1

Ask your question

Type anything. Grainulator extracts parameters, audience, and constraints. It searches for prior research automatically.

2

Research and challenge

Multiple angles explored in parallel. Every finding becomes a typed claim. Then /challenge stress-tests them adversarially.

3

Compile the brief

The compiler catches contradictions, flags weak evidence, and produces your decision document. Only what passes the gate ships.

Evidence system

Five tiers. No hand-waving.

Every claim is graded by how it was verified. The compiler enforces minimums.

1
stated
Stakeholder said it. No verification.
2
web
Found online. Not independently verified.
3
documented
In source code, official docs, or ADRs.
4
tested
Verified via prototype or benchmark.
5
production
Measured from live production systems.
