How it works

You ask. Grainulator delivers.

Five stages, completely automatic. You see "researching 3 angles in parallel." You get a compiled brief.

1

Ask your question

Type anything: "Should we migrate to Postgres?" Grainulator extracts parameters and searches for prior research.

2

Research and challenge

Multiple angles explored in parallel. Findings are gathered, challenged adversarially, and corroborated with evidence.

3

Compile the brief

The compiler validates everything -- catches contradictions, flags weak evidence, and produces your decision document.
